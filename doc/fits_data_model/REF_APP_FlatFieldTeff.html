REF_APP_FlatFieldTeff

Brief: Calibration product : Calculated Flat Field frames for different Teff
Description: There are two images per calculated flat field. The first is the flat field itself, the second is an error map. In the data cube first all the Flat Fields are stored than their error maps. See also column DATA_TYPE in the attached REF_APP_FlatFieldTeffMetadata table. The flat fields are normalised to their average value. One pixel in the flat field correspond to one CCD pixel.
