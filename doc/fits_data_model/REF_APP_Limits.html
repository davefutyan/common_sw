REF_APP_Limits

Brief: Hard and soft limits of HK parameters and derived parameters
Description: Stores the limits of HK parameters and derived parameters. Several of such tables can be valid at the same time. They are distinguished by their data name, see keyword DTA_NAME. They are used by the limit_check program.
