EXT_DRFT_StarCatalogue

Brief: CHEOPS Star Catalogue
Description: One table list the target star and the background stars of one visit. It is derived from CHEOPS Star Catalogue. First the EXT_DRFT_StarCatalogue is created by the star_catalogueExtraction tool. visit_combination then creates the EXT_PRE_StarCatalogu and sets the VISITCTR.
