AUX_RES_ObtUtcCorrelation

Brief: Pairs of UTC_N and OBT_N times
Description: The pairs of UTC_N and OBT_N define the correlation between these two time units. Correlations at times between two pairs have to be interpolated: UTC = OFFSET + TC_OFFSET + UTC_N + GRADIENT * (OBT - OBT_N). According to MOC-SOC IDC the TC_OFFSET and GRADIENT are valid from this data point (UTC_TIMESTAMP) until the next data point (UTC_TIMESTAMP), which defines the next the TC_OFFSET and GRADIENT. There will be one OBT-UTC Correlation file per pass. About 5 to 10 time correlation records are expected per pass.
