SOC_APP_VisitDataTimeOut

SOC_APP_VisitDataTimeOut

Brief: Defines the Time Out of the Visit Data.
Description: The Time Out of Visit Data defines how long the processing of visit data shall be suspended to wait for missing data. The time out starts when the first TM data of a visit arrives as SOC. The Data Reduction procession shall start anyhow if the missing data are still not available at SOC after waiting for this time out period.
